# Sentinel Privacy Policy

*Last updated: July 11, 2026*

Sentinel is designed to work entirely offline. The app:

- **Collects no personal data.** No accounts, no sign-in, no identifiers.
- **Sends nothing to the internet.** The app communicates exclusively with your own Sentinel base station over a direct, local Bluetooth Low Energy connection. There are no servers, no analytics, no tracking SDKs, and no third-party services.
- **Stores data only on your device.** Alert history and settings stay on your iPhone and are never transmitted anywhere.
- **Uses Bluetooth** solely to connect to your Sentinel base station. This is why iOS asks for Bluetooth permission.
- **Uses notifications** solely to deliver alerts generated by your own hardware.

The Sentinel sensor hardware performs passive Bluetooth scanning in your own monitored area. It observes only anonymous, publicly broadcast radio advertisements, does not connect to any detected device, does not collect device contents, and retains no identifying information. Detection events contain only a node identifier, a device count, and a signal-strength estimate.
